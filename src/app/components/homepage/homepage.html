Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

Is my data private?

Yes, 100%. All processing happens on your device. No data is ever sent to the cloud, ensuring complete privacy.

What technology does it use?

Resilience AI runs on cutting-edge local LLMs like Llama 3.2 and Gemma 2, with immersive 3D VRM avatars powered by WebGPU.

What do I need to run it?

A modern browser like Chrome or Edge that supports WebGPU. You can check the browser compatibility section for more details.

Can I customize my AI coach?

Absolutely! You can choose from various avatars and even upload your own VRM model for a truly personalized experience.
